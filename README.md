# Pixel Needle ✨  
## Modern Cross Stitch Patterns for Creative Minds

Welcome to **Pixel Needle**, a modern online shop dedicated to unique and stylish cross stitch patterns in digital PDF format.

Our designs combine clean pixel-art aesthetics with contemporary themes, making stitching both relaxing and inspiring.

---

## 🧵 What We Offer

- Instant digital PDF downloads  
- Original pixel-art cross stitch designs  
- Clear and easy-to-follow charts  
- Modern and minimalist style  
- Suitable for beginners and experienced stitchers  

---

## 💡 About the Project

Pixel Needle was created to bring a fresh and modern touch to the timeless art of cross stitch.

Each pattern is carefully designed to balance simplicity, clarity, and visual impact — making every stitch satisfying.
